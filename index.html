About me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Stockholm School of Economics and the University of Copenhagen. My main research interests are in health economics and applied microeconomics. I currently work on topics related to physician decision-making and antibiotic prescribing and resistance.

I received my PhD in Economics from the University of Copenhagen in 2023. Previously, I was based at the DIW Berlin Graduate Center/Berlin School of Economics, and the University of Zurich.
